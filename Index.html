ABOUT US

WHAT IS HACKtoLEARN?

Our Mission

HACKtoLEARN is a global hackathon initiative designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. We believe the best way to learn is by doing, and our events provide the perfect platform for developers, designers, and innovators to create, collaborate, and grow.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every aspiring developer has access to resources, mentorship, and a community that empowers them to turn their ideas into reality. Through our hackathons, workshops, and networking events, we're building the next generation of tech innovators.

Who Can Participate?

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, HACKtoLEARN welcomes participants of all skill levels. Our tracks are designed to challenge beginners and experts alike, with dedicated mentorship to guide you through the process.

Who can participate in HACKtoLEARN?
Anyone passionate about learning and innovation can join HACKtoLEARN! We welcome students, professionals, and hobbyists from all backgrounds and skill levels.
Is there a registration fee?
No! HACKtoLEARN is absolutely free to participate. We believe in making hackathons accessible to everyone who wants to learn and create.
Do I need a team to participate?
You can join solo or form a team of up to 4 members. If you're looking for teammates, join our Discord server where you can connect with other participants.
What are the prizes?
Exciting cash rewards, exclusive goodies, internship opportunities, and certificates await the winners! We also have special prizes for different categories including Best Girls Team.

We're looking for groups of 1‚Äì4 heroes. Solo adventurers are welcome ‚Äî or gather your buddies and hack together! Teams can be formed before or during the event.
Will there be mentorship available?
Yes! We have industry experts and experienced developers who will be available throughout the hackathon to guide you, answer questions, and help you overcome technical challenges.
What should I bring to the hackathon?
For virtual participants: A laptop, stable internet connection, and lots of enthusiasm! For offline participants: Additionally bring chargers, personal items, and an open mind ready to learn.
Can beginners participate?
Absolutely! HACKtoLEARN is designed for all skill levels. We have beginner-friendly tracks, workshops, and mentors specifically to help newcomers get started with their first hackathon project.
